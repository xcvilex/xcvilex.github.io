When You Realize You’ve Been Abandoning Yourself Longer Than Anyone Else Has

You ever sit down and realize the longest, most consistent ghosting situation you’ve ever been in… is with yourself?

I used to think I had abandonment issues because of other people. Turns out, I just got really good at pre-abandoning myself so no one else had to bother.

I didn’t speak up. Didn’t rest. Didn’t ask for help. Didn’t say “ouch” when it hurt.

Now I’m learning how to stay. With me. Even when I’m needy. Even when I disappoint myself.

That’s what healing looks like. Sometimes it’s just not leaving the room when your own heart is breaking.
